Canverse

The Problem

Creating digital music today is far too complicated. The common software tools offered today are made with multiple confusing widgets, dials and options that can make creating music difficult for even an in-depth user.

While creating original music is an artistic, creative and free flowing process, most modern software limits this by enforcing a rigid left to right timeline approach. This is not well-suited for playing free form instruments such as synthesizers, and also disrupts the creation of a rhythmic beat, forcing the user to unnaturally reason about how to space out notes when they would rather just tap their beat out.

Our goal is to break the uniform structure of most musical software tools offered today and provide the freedom to make creating digital music natural and fun.
